Current Researchers

Caleb Harper

Caleb is currently an urban food systems researcher and writing his masters thesis on the subject of High-density / Low-income housing in developing countries at the MIT School of Architecture and the MIT Media Lab.


Through thoughtful analysis, design and synthesis of housing, health, food, economics and locale, Caleb is part of a new generation of multi-faceted thought leaders offering nuanced solutions for urban development in a global context. Caleb has worked professionally and academically in the research and implementation of domestic projects in Texas, Nevada, Florida and Massachusetts as well as internationally in The United Kingdom, Ecuador, Japan, Singapore, Argentina, Brazil and India.
